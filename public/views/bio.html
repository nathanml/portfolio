About Me

I am a software engineer with four years of professional experience designing and developing cloud-based technology solutions. I specialize in object-oriented programming, APIs, and distributed systems. As a tech lead, I have delivered award-winning enterprise services to production.

Currently, I work on developing enterprise cloud-based microservices at Citizens Financial Group, where I am tech lead of a pod that has worked on several core banking initiatives, tackling complex problems such as electronic signature capture, claim disputes, and rapid home equity loan approval.

Additionally, I am completing my Masters of Science in Computer Science from Tufts University School of Engineering.

Outside of work, I love to play piano, go hiking, volunteer, read, go to movies, and cuddle with my very spoiled kitty, Evie.
